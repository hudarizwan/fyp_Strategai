## One-Line Project Summary
StrategAI analyzes wholesale and retail market data, recommends profitable pricing, and generates evidence-based marketing strategies for Pakistani e-commerce products.

## Problem Statement
- Online sellers often guess pricing without strong market evidence.
- Market data is scattered across wholesale and retail platforms.
- Manual analysis takes time and still misses pricing, competition, and demand patterns.
- Pakistani e-commerce needs a practical decision support system that is fast, explainable, and market-aware.

## Motivation
- E-commerce competition is increasing in Pakistan.
- Sellers need faster pricing decisions.
- AI can help turn scraped market data into useful business intelligence.
- The project is useful because it supports real product selection, pricing, and launch decisions.

## Objectives
- Collect wholesale and retail product data from the web.
- Clean and normalize the scraped data.
- Recommend buy and sell prices using analytics models.
- Generate marketing strategy based on market evidence.
- Provide confidence, low-sample warnings, and explainable reasoning.
- Present a clear executive summary for decision-making.

### Suggested workflow caption

### Short method description
StrategAI first scrapes wholesale and retail marketplace data, then cleans and groups it, runs pricing analytics to recommend buy and sell values, creates a marketing plan from the resulting market intelligence, and finally produces a decision summary and report.
